Part-time Nanny

Wanted: Part-time nanny on Mon, Wed, Fri from 9AM - 5PM in central Phoenix.

About the job

The job is looking after our baby daughter, Lottie! We need someone who can come to our home in central Phoenix and watch her during the day while my wife and I work. You'll feed her, change her, bounce her on her bouncer, put her down for a nap, read her a book, etc., baby stuff.

Lottie was born in August so she'll be about 3 months old by November, which is when we need you to start (that is when mom has to go back to work). My wife and I both work from home, so we'll be around during the day, but in our offices (one's in the house, one's out back). You and Lottie will have the run of the kitchen, living room and nursery.

Days/Hours

We would like you to be at our home from 9AM to 5PM on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays. The days and hours are somewhat flexible, so we may be able to work with your schedule where possible.

When does the job start?

Monday November 8th - that is when Mom has to go back to work. However, we are going to visit Grandma on Friday November 12th. So the first week is actually only Monday and Wednesday.

How much does the job pay?

The starting wage is $16 per hour. If everything goes well, you like us, we like you, etc., we will increase that to $17 per hour after 3 months.

COVID Stuff

My wife and I are vaccinated against COVID-19, and we would like our daughter's nanny to be vaccinated, too. Once we decided we are a good fit for one another, we will require that you send us a copy of your vaccination card (we're happy to provide ours, too).

How to Apply

  • Resume with relevant experience
